About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Istanbul, we set out with a simple mission to build a service we wanted to use ourselves. Eight years on and we’re present in 8 more countries - France, Germany, Italy, the Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, United Kingdom, and USA - delivered more than 200 million orders, and acquired a number of other ultra-fast companies such as Block, Frichti, Gorillas, and Weezy.

Today we’re on a mission to free up time for the moments that matter, delivering happiness to local communities, and enabling everyone to live life to the fullest.

Getir Means Bring

In Turkish, the word ‘getir’ means bring - and bringing happiness is what we do. We provide high-quality, fresh groceries, delivered sustainably in minutes.

To achieve this, we’ve built our business model on the principles of operational excellence, speed, and trust.
